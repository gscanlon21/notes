Avoid clothing, bedding, towels, upholstery, bags, shoes, and other items made with virgin (new):
- Polyester
- Nylon
- Acrylic
- Microfibers
- Microsuede

Choose natural fibers instead, such as:
- Organic cotton
- Organic wool
- Hemp
- Jute
- Kapok
