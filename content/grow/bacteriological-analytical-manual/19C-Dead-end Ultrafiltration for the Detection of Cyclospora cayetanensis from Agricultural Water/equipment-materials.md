# Recovery and Detection of Cyclospora cayetanensis from Ultrafilters
# _Part II

## Laboratory Facilities 
- A laboratory bench for the ultrafilter backflushing and concentration procedures. 
- A laboratory bench or hood for the DNA extraction procedure. 
- Separate workstations for each step of the qPCR protocol to allow an efficient workflow and eliminate the potential for positive results due to laboratory contamination.
