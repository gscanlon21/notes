# Appendix 4

## 1. Food
- Milk and Milk Products:
  - Whole Milk
  - Cheese[^1]:
    - Soft Cheese
    - Hard Cheese
    - Cheese Products
- Produce:
  - Leafy Greens (includes Bagged Ready-to-Eat Fresh Cut Vegetables): Basil, Cilantro, Green Onions, Loose-Leaf Lettuce, Spinach, and Parsley
  - Melons (e.g. Cantaloupes)
  - Papaya
  - Tomatoes
  - Cucumber
  - Peppers: Sweet Pepper, Hot pepper
  - Sprouts (e.g. Alfalfa, Clover)
- Seafood:
  - Finfish: Fresh, Frozen, Smoked, Salted
  - Crustaceans: Shrimp, Crab & Crab Meat, Lobster, Crayfish
  - Shellfish: Oyster, Clam, Scallop, Mussel
- Shell Eggs
- Spices
  - Pepper: White, Black, Red 
  - Cloves, Oregano, Cinnamon 
- Water 
  - Processing water (e.g. Sprout Irrigation Water) 
  - Bottled Water 

## 2. Feed 
- Pet Food/Treats 
- Animal Feeds: Poultry, Cattle, Swine, Horse, Medicated Feed, Feed for Minor Species, Feed Ingredients 


[^1]: Cheeses made from unpasteurized milk are of particular interest.
