- Water 
  - Deionized water (for washing produce [dH2O]) 
  - Sterile deionized water (for PCR procedure) 
- Envirochek™ elution buffer (0.01M Tris, pH 7.4 ; 0.001 M EDTA; 1% SDS) 
- Silicone vacuum grease 
- Albumin, bovine (BSA) (Sigma, A-7030) 
- Celite (Sigma, C-8656) 
- Polyvinyl polypyrrolidone (PVPP) (Sigma, P-6755)

- NET-BSA buffer-NET buffer containing 1% (w/v) BSA  
- 20% celite in NET-BSA buffer (w/v) 
- 10% PVPP (w/v) in dH2O 
- Dynabeads anti-Cryptosporidium Kit (Dynal Biotech Inc, USA) 
- Hydrofluor Combo Detection Kit for Cryptosporidium and Giardia (Strategic Diagnostics, Inc.) 
- 0.1 N HCl 
- 0.1 N NaOH 
- Immersion oil 
- Clear fingernail polish, slide compound, paraffin wax or equivalent 
- FTA Purification Buffer (Whatman Biosciences) 
- FTA filter wash buffer: 0.01 M Tris, Ph 8.0; 0.1 mM EDTA 
- DNA Primers-See Table 1 in PCR Section
