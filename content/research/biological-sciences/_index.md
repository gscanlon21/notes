# Terms

**In Vivo**
_Within the living_
: It refers to work that’s performed in a whole, living organism.

**In Vitro**
_Within the glass_
: When something is performed in vitro, it happens outside of a living organism.

**In Situ**
_In its original place_
: Something that’s performed in situ means that it’s observed in its natural context, but outside of a living organism.
