# Articles

**Impaired p53/CEP-1 is associated with lifespan extension through an age-related imbalance in the energy metabolism of C. elegans**
Yanase et al. 2017
_[Brief Report](/doi/10.1111/gtc.12540.pdf)_ 
: "These results suggest that impaired p53/CEP- 1 leads to an imbalance in the age- related energy metabolic alteration between mitochondrial oxidative phosphorylation and aerobic glycolysis and plays an important role in the extension of both intact and adaptive lifespans."
