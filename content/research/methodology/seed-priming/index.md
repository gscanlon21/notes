# Terminology

**Hydropriming**
: Partial hydration or limited time of hydration.

**Osmopriming**
: Controlled hydration using an osmoticum.

**Osmoticum**
: Any substance that acts to supplement osmotic pressure in a plant or a culture of plant cells.
: > Polyethylene Glycol (PEG)


# Research Articles

**Accelerated Germination by Osmotic Seed Treatment**
Heydecker et al. 1973
_[Research Article](/doi/10.1038/246042A0.pdf)_
: Osmotic pretreatment of seeds increases the rates and uniformity of germination.
: "The combination of -10 bar and 15 C for 11 days, however, was nearly as successful in increasing the rate of germination of the seed population as the best treatment."


# Methodology

**The Osmotic Potential of Polyethylene Glycol 6000**
Michel et al. 1973
_[Methodology](/doi/10.1104/pp.51.5.914.pdf)_
: The data were found to fit the following equation:
: Ψ = -(1.18 × 10<sup>-2</sup>)C - (1.18 × 10<sup>-4</sup>)C² + (2.67 × 10<sup>-4</sup>)CT + (8.39 × 10<sup>-7</sup>)C²T
: **Ψ**
  : Osmotic potential (bar)
  
  **C**
  : Concentration PEG-6000 (g/kg H,O)

  **T**
  : Temperature (°C)
