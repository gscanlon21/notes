# Rockwool
1. Dilute with water or add appropriate amount of acid to 5.5-6 when checked by pH paper.
1. Place cutting and seeds into the cubes using the pre-made planting hole.
1. Add plant food and irrigate as needed to keep the blocks moist, but not excessively.
1. Transplant into soil or other system when roots extend 1-inch below the cube.


# Rapid Rooter
1. Soak plugs in water or mild nutrient solution to re-hydrate. 
1. Place seed or cutting in center hole of plug.
1. Keep about a quarter-inch of water in bottom of tray to maintain plug moisture.
1. When roots appear, plugs are ready to be transplanted.

## For Germination
1. Soak the Plugs
  - Submerge Rapid Rooter plugs in distilled water or weak nutrient solution for 30 seconds to 1 minute until fully saturated.
1. Plant Seeds
  - Place 1-2 seeds in the center hole of each plug. Cover with a small piece of the plug material to maintain darkness.
1. Set Up Tray
  - Place plugs in tray insert with about ½ inch of water in the bottom. Cover with humidity dome to maintain moisture.
1. Provide Light
  - Place under low-intensity grow light and maintain 70-80°F temperature for optimal germination. Roots typically emerge in 3-7 days.
