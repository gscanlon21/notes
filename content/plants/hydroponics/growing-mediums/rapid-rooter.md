## For Germination
1. Soak the Plugs
  - Submerge Rapid Rooter plugs in distilled water or weak nutrient solution for 30 seconds to 1 minute until fully saturated.
1. Plant Seeds
  - Place 1-2 seeds in the center hole of each plug. Cover with a small piece of the plug material to maintain darkness.
1. Set Up Tray
  - Place plugs in tray insert with about ½ inch of water in the bottom. Cover with humidity dome to maintain moisture.
1. Provide Light
  - Place under low-intensity grow light and maintain 70-80°F temperature for optimal germination. Roots typically emerge in 3-7 days.
