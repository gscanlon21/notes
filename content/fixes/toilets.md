##### Chain Coming Off the Plug
Use zip ties so the chain doesn't come off the plug.

##### Toilet Fill Valve Not Shutting Off[^1]
1. Shutoff the water at the angle stop.
1. Flush and drain the water out of the tank.
1. Remove the clip from the water-level stem.
1. Push down and turn the valve cap a quarter turn to the left.
  - The clip to the stem may need to be lifted in order to turn the valve cap.
1. Gently turn on the water with a cup over the pipe to clean out mineral deposits.
1. Clean or replace the seal under the valve cap. They can get old and deteriorate.
  - In a pinch, plumber's tape can wrapped around the outside of the seal to bulk it up.
