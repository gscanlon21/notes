# Capsulitis

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Stretch your calves daily! 
> Tight calves will lift the heel off the ground prematurely forcing weight onto the toes.

- Make sure most of the force through the foot is going into the big toe metatarsal. Good walking posture.
- Wear shoes with a good forefoot rocker so the ball of the root doesn't bend and the ligament isn't stretched.
- Make sure calves are loose. Tight calves will lift the heel off the ground prematurely forcing weight onto the toes.
- After inflammation is in check, strengthen the metatarsal ligaments with toe flexion exercises.


# Metatarsal Fractures
Need to offload some of the pressure on the metatarsal heads. Try orthotics or metatarsal pads to elevate the metatarsal dome. 

> [!WARNING] 
> The metatarsal dome is back from the metatarsal heads. Putting pressure on the metatarsal heads by mistake will increase the pressure on them and make the pain worse.
