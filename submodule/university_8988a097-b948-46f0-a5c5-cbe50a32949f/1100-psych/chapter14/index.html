They should have certain ethical guidelines

Psychotherapists work to benefit their clients while trying to do no harm. Psychotherapists establish trust with their clients and maintain standards of professionalism. Beneficence and nonmaleficenc e. Psychotherapists communicate honestly and truthfully with their clients. Fidelity and responsibility. Psychotherapists engage in fair and equitable practices. Integrity. Psychotherapists recognize that each individual has a right to autonomy. Justice. Rights and dignity. What am I looking for?
