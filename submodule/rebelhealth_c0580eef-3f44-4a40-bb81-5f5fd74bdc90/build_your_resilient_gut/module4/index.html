    Key Points

    • Walking for 15-20 minutes after meals is really good for you.
      • Movement is critical for allowing digestive processes to function properly.
    • It normally takes food 2-3 hours to get past to the stomach where it can be fermented by SIBO bacteria.
      • If bloating occurs within those first few hours where food is in the stomach, then it's likely not SIBO.
