    English is a germanic language with lots of latin vocabulary. Spanish is a modern latin language. Latin words in English can be converted to Spanish very easily.

    Words ending in al tend to come from latin, so they can be converted almost verbatim (with a different accent).

    In spanish, vowels are pronounced exactly as they sound. Pronounce the vowels as they are written.
